# Thai-Style Coconut Ginger Soup with Prawns & Snow Peas

## Ingredients (Serves 4)

- 400 g raw prawns, peeled and deveined
- 1 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, finely grated
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp red curry paste
- 400 ml (1 can) coconut milk
- 3 cups chicken stock
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tsp brown sugar
- 150 g snow peas, trimmed
- 200 g mushrooms, sliced (optional)
- 1 tbsp lime juice (plus extra to taste)
- Fresh coriander, to serve
- Fresh red chilli, sliced (optional)
- Cooked rice or rice noodles (optional, for serving)

---

## Method

### 1. Build the Base
1. Heat 1 tbsp vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add 1 tbsp grated ginger and 2 minced garlic cloves. Cook for 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
3. Stir in 1 tbsp red curry paste and cook for 1 minute to release flavour.

### 2. Create the Broth
1. Pour in 400 ml coconut milk and 3 cups chicken stock. Stir to combine.
2. Add 1 tbsp fish sauce and 1 tsp brown sugar.
3. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 5 minutes.

### 3. Add Vegetables & Prawns
1. Add 200 g sliced mushrooms (if using) and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
2. Add 150 g snow peas and cook for 1 minute.
3. Add 400 g raw prawns and simmer for 2–3 minutes until pink and just cooked through.

### 4. Finish
1. Turn off heat and stir in 1 tbsp lime juice.
2. Taste and adjust with extra lime juice or fish sauce if needed.

### 5. Serve
1. Ladle into bowls as is, or over cooked rice or rice noodles.
2. Top with fresh coriander and sliced red chilli if desired.
3. Serve immediately while hot.
