# Creamy Mushroom Soup with Turmeric & Thyme

## Ingredients (Serves 4)

- 600 g mushrooms, sliced
- 2 tbsp butter or oil
- 2 onions, finely chopped
- 1/2 leek, sliced
- 1 large carrot, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp fresh thyme (or 1 tsp dried)
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 1/2 cups vegetable or chicken stock (600 ml)
- 3/4 cup milk (200 ml)
- 2 tbsp crème fraîche or cream
- Salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Fresh thyme or parsley, to serve

---

## Method

### 1. Cook the Aromatics
1. Melt **2 tbsp butter** in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add **2 finely chopped onions** and **2 minced garlic cloves** and cook for about **5 minutes** until soft and fragrant.

### 2. Cook the Vegetables
1. Add **600 g sliced mushrooms**, **1/2 sliced leek**, and **1 diced carrot**.
2. Stir in **1 tsp turmeric**, **1 tbsp fresh thyme**, **1 tsp Worcestershire sauce**, and a pinch of **salt and black pepper**.
3. Cook for about **5 minutes** until the mushrooms soften and release their moisture.

### 3. Simmer the Soup
1. Pour in **2 1/2 cups vegetable or chicken stock (600 ml)** and **3/4 cup milk (200 ml)**.
2. Bring the soup to a gentle boil, then reduce heat and simmer for **10 minutes**.

### 4. Blend
1. Blend the soup with an immersion blender until smooth, or leave slightly chunky if preferred.
2. Let the soup simmer gently for another **2–3 minutes**.

### 5. Finish
1. Stir in **2 tbsp crème fraîche or cream**.
2. Taste and adjust with **salt and black pepper**.

### 6. Serve
1. Ladle the soup into bowls.
2. Top with **fresh thyme or parsley** and extra black pepper.
3. Serve hot with crusty bread.
