# Hearty Lentil Soup (Collard Greens / Kale)

## Ingredients (Serves 4–6)

- 1/4 cup (60 ml) extra virgin olive oil
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- 1 large can (28 oz / ~800 g) diced tomatoes, lightly drained
- 1 cup (200 g) brown or green lentils, rinsed
- 4 cups (1 litre) vegetable broth
- 2 cups (500 ml) water
- 1 tsp salt (plus more to taste)
- Pinch red pepper flakes
- Black pepper, to taste
- 1 cup chopped collard greens or kale (ribs removed)
- 1–2 tbsp lemon juice

---

## Method

### 1. Cook the Base
1. Heat **1/4 cup olive oil (60 ml)** in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add **1 chopped onion** and **2 chopped carrots**, and cook for **5–7 minutes** until softened.
3. Add **4 minced garlic cloves**, **2 tsp cumin**, **1 tsp curry powder**, and **1/2 tsp thyme**, and cook for **1 minute** until fragrant.

### 2. Build the Soup
1. Add **1 large can diced tomatoes (28 oz / ~800 g)** and stir to combine.
2. Add **1 cup lentils (200 g)**, **4 cups vegetable broth (1 litre)**, and **2 cups water (500 ml)**.
3. Stir in **1 tsp salt**, a **pinch of red pepper flakes**, and some **black pepper**.

### 3. Simmer
1. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
2. Simmer uncovered for **25–30 minutes**, stirring occasionally, until lentils are tender.

### 4. Add Greens
1. Stir in **1 cup chopped collard greens or kale**.
2. Cook for **5 minutes** until wilted and tender.

### 5. Finish
1. Stir in **1–2 tbsp lemon juice**.
2. Taste and adjust with more **salt**, **pepper**, or **lemon juice** as needed.

### 6. Serve
1. Ladle into bowls and serve hot.
