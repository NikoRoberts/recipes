# Hearty Lentil Soup

## Ingredients (Serves 4–6)

- 1/4 cup (60 ml) olive oil
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- 1 can (400 g) diced tomatoes
- 1 cup (200 g) dried brown or green lentils, rinsed
- 4 cups (1 litre) vegetable broth
- 2 cups (500 ml) water
- 1 tsp salt (plus more to taste)
- Black pepper, to taste
- 1–2 tbsp lemon juice (to finish)
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

---

## Method

### 1. Cook the Base
1. Heat **1/4 cup olive oil (60 ml)** in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add **1 chopped onion**, **2 diced carrots**, and **2 diced celery stalks** and cook for about **5–7 minutes** until softened.
3. Add **4 minced garlic cloves**, **2 tsp cumin**, **1 tsp curry powder**, and **1/2 tsp thyme**, then cook for **1 minute** until fragrant.

### 2. Build the Soup
1. Pour in **1 can diced tomatoes (400 g)** and stir to combine.
2. Add **1 cup dried lentils (200 g)**, **4 cups vegetable broth (1 litre)**, and **2 cups water (500 ml)**.
3. Add **1 tsp salt** and a few grinds of **black pepper**.

### 3. Simmer
1. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
2. Simmer uncovered for **25–30 minutes**, stirring occasionally, until the lentils are tender and the soup thickens.

### 4. Adjust Texture
1. For a creamier texture, blend a portion of the soup using a stick blender (about 2–3 cups of the soup), then stir back in.

### 5. Finish
1. Stir in **1–2 tbsp lemon juice**.
2. Taste and adjust with more **salt**, **pepper**, or **lemon juice** as needed.

### 6. Serve
1. Ladle into bowls.
2. Top with **fresh parsley** if using.
3. Serve hot, optionally with crusty bread.
