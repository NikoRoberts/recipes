# Mushroom Lentil Stew

## Ingredients (Serves 6–8)

- 6 tbsp olive oil
- 2 yellow onions, diced
- 2 cups sliced carrot
- 2 cups sliced celery
- 32 oz (900 g) baby bella mushrooms, halved
- 12 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
- 4 tsp dried thyme
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp dried sage
- 4 tbsp flour
- 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
- 4 tbsp soy sauce
- 32 oz (900 g) baby yellow potatoes, halved
- 2 cups uncooked split red lentils
- 2 cans (14.5 oz / 410 g each) tomato sauce
- 6 cups vegetable broth
- 4 bay leaves
- Kosher salt
- Fresh cracked black pepper
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

---

## Method

### 1. Cook the Vegetables
1. Heat **6 tbsp olive oil** in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add **2 diced onions**, **2 cups sliced carrot**, and **2 cups sliced celery** along with a couple pinches of **salt and black pepper**.
3. Cook for **8 minutes**, stirring occasionally, until softened.

### 2. Add Mushrooms & Herbs
1. Add **32 oz halved baby bella mushrooms**, **12 thinly sliced garlic cloves**, **4 tsp dried thyme**, **2 tsp dried oregano**, and **1 tsp dried sage**.
2. Add another couple pinches of **salt and black pepper**.
3. Cook for **3–4 minutes**, stirring frequently, until mushrooms begin to soften.

### 3. Build the Base
1. Sprinkle in **4 tbsp flour** and stir well to coat the vegetables.
2. Cook for **1 minute**, stirring frequently.
3. Pour in **1/2 cup balsamic vinegar** and **4 tbsp soy sauce**.
4. Stir well, scraping up anything stuck to the bottom of the pot.

### 4. Simmer the Stew
1. Add **32 oz halved baby yellow potatoes**, **2 cups split red lentils**, **2 cans tomato sauce (14.5 oz each)**, **6 cups vegetable broth**, and **4 bay leaves**.
2. Season generously with **salt and black pepper**.
3. Bring to a simmer over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally so the lentils do not stick.

### 5. Finish Cooking
1. Reduce heat to low and simmer gently for **10–15 minutes** until the potatoes are fork-tender and the lentils are soft.
2. Taste and adjust seasoning with more **salt and black pepper** as needed.

### 6. Serve
1. Remove the **4 bay leaves**.
2. Ladle into bowls and top with **fresh parsley** if using.
3. Serve hot on its own or with mashed potatoes or crusty bread.
