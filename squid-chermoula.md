### 1. Make the chermoula  
Pound the **cumin**, **garlic**, **saffron water**, **ginger**, **paprika**, **turmeric**, and **cayenne** in a mortar and pestle until a paste forms.  
Gradually work in **3 tbsp olive oil** to finish the chermoula.

### 2. Marinate the squid  
Place **500 g squid** in a bowl and coat thoroughly with the chermoula.  
Cover and marinate for **30 minutes**.

### 3. Start the stew  
Heat **2 tbsp olive oil** in a tagine or heavy-based frying pan over mediumâ€“low heat.  
Add the **chopped onion** and cook for **5 minutes**, until soft and translucent.

### 4. Cook the squid  
Add the marinated squid to the pan.  
Increase heat to mediumâ€“high and cook, stirring, for **5 minutes**.

### 5. Add tomatoes & seasoning  
Add the **tomatoes**, **honey**, **lemon juice**, and **sea salt**.  
Bring to the boil, then reduce heat to a gentle simmer.

### 6. Slow cook  
Cover and cook for **about 1 hour**, until the squid is tender and the sauce rich.

### 7. Finish with potatoes  
Add the **steamed potatoes** and gently combine.  
Warm through for **5 minutes**.

### 8. Serve  
Divide between four plates and finish with **coriander leaves** and **fresh chilli slices**.
