# Crispy Gochujang Chicken Thighs with Pan Sauce, Pickled Cucumber & Crispy Potatoes

## Ingredients (Serves 2)

### Chicken
- 4 skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs
- Salt
- 2–3 tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste)

### Pan Sauce
- 1 shallot, finely diced
- 1 tsp ginger, grated
- 1–2 cloves garlic, grated
- 1–2 tbsp gochujang
- 60 ml soju (or sake / white wine)
- 120 ml chicken stock (or kombu dashi)
- 2–3 tbsp pickled cucumber juice (from below)
- 1–2 tbsp butter

### Quick Pickled Cucumber
- 1–2 cucumbers, thinly sliced
- 2–3 tbsp rice wine vinegar
- 1–2 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp Korean chili flakes (gochugaru)
- 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds
- 1–2 tbsp spring onions, sliced

### Crispy Potatoes
- 400–500g new potatoes
- Olive oil
- Salt

### Gochujang Mayo
- 2 tbsp mayo (Kewpie preferred)
- 1 tsp gochujang
- 1 tsp rice wine vinegar
- 1/2 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp honey

### Garnish
- Sesame seeds
- Fresh coriander (optional)

---

## Instructions

### 1. Quick Pickled Cucumber
- Combine cucumber, vinegar, sugar, and salt.
- Mix and let sit for **15 minutes**.
- Add chili flakes, sesame seeds, and spring onions.
- Reserve some of the pickling liquid for the sauce.

---

### 2. Crispy Potatoes
- Boil potatoes in salted water until tender.
- Drain and place on a tray.
- Crush lightly using another tray or glass.
- Drizzle with olive oil and salt.
- Roast at **200°C** for **25–30 minutes** until golden and crispy.

---

### 3. Cook the Chicken
- Lightly salt chicken thighs.
- Pat dry just before cooking.

- Place in a **cold pan, skin-side down (no oil)**.
- Turn heat to low (about 2/10).
- Slowly render fat and cook until skin is golden and crisp.
- Flip once they release easily.

- Brush generously with gochujang.
- Transfer to oven and cook for **5–10 minutes** until internal temp reaches **75°C**.
- Rest the chicken.

---

### 4. Make the Pan Sauce
- Pour off ~80% of the fat (reserve for future cooking).

- On low heat, add shallots and cook until translucent.
- Add ginger → cook 1 minute.
- Add garlic → cook until fragrant.

- Stir in gochujang (keep heat low to avoid burning).
- Deglaze with soju (or alternative).
- Reduce until slightly thick/jammy.

- Add stock and simmer until reduced by half.
- Add reserved pickle juice and reduce again by half.

- Turn off heat and whisk in butter until glossy.

---

### 5. Gochujang Mayo
- Mix mayo, gochujang, vinegar, sesame oil, and honey.
- Adjust to taste (sweet/spicy balance).

---

### 6. Assemble
- Spoon sauce onto plate.
- Place chicken on top.
- Add crispy potatoes and pickled cucumber on the side.
- Drizzle or dollop gochujang mayo.

---

## Notes
- Starting chicken in a cold pan renders fat for ultra-crispy skin.
- Pickle juice adds acidity that balances the rich sauce.
- Keep heat low when cooking gochujang—it can burn easily.
- This dish is all about contrast: **crispy, rich, tangy, and spicy**.
